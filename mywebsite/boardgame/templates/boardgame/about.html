About BoardGameMeet

Our Mission

Our mission is to make it easy for board game lovers to connect with others who share their passion. We understand how important it is to find a community where you can enjoy your favorite games. That’s why we’ve created a space where you can:

  • Join Communities: Find and join local groups that share your interest in board games.
  • Discover Events: Stay updated on upcoming events and game nights in your area.
  • Connect with Friends: Meet new people, make friends, and share your love for board games.

The Story Behind BoardGameMeet

BoardGameMeet was created by Darby Christy, a software development student at the University of Glasgow. Darby’s journey began when she moved to Glasgow and wanted to find a way to connect with her new community. Inspired by her own experience, Darby created an application that would bring board game enthusiasts together, making it easier for them to find game groups and participate in local events.

How It Works

  • Find & Join Groups: Search for board game groups in your area and become a member.
  • Attend Events: Browse and join events where you can meet other players and enjoy your favorite games.
  • Nominate & Vote: Participate in game nominations and voting to help plan future events.
  • Create & Manage: Create your own groups and events and manage game sign-ups and nominations with ease.
